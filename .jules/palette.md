## 2025-05-14 - Search Discoverability & Reset Patterns
**Learning:** Users can feel "stuck" in empty search states if they have multiple active filters (e.g., a search query + a tag). Providing a prominent "Clear all filters" button in the empty state, alongside a "Clear search" icon in the input, significantly improves the perceived reliability and ease of use of the catalog.
**Action:** Always include reset actions in filter-heavy interfaces, specifically targeting the "empty results" edge case.
